About Us

At Khutti Ma Jutti, we specialize in traditional Punjabi juttis that celebrate cultural elegance and craftsmanship. Our collection features beautifully handcrafted juttis inspired by classic designs like Jumka and Payal juttis, adorned with intricate hand embroidery. Each pair is thoughtfully made with both comfort and style in mind, offering options with soft cushioned soles as well as durable leather soles. Whether for festive occasions or everyday wear, Khutti Ma Jutti brings you authentic, stylish footwear that honors tradition while keeping your feet happy.
